About Universal Smart AI

Our Mission

Universal Smart AI is dedicated to making quality education accessible to everyone. We believe that AI-powered tutoring should be free and available to all students, regardless of their background or location.

How It Works

Our AI tutor uses Google's advanced Gemini AI to help students learn any subject. Simply ask a question, and our AI provides detailed, personalized explanations. You can also upload documents, draw geometry diagrams, generate flashcards, and take quizzes to reinforce your learning.

Free vs Premium

We offer unlimited free questions with ads to keep the service accessible. For those who prefer an ad-free experience, we offer a premium subscription for $4.99/month. All features are included in both tiers.

Our Team

Universal Smart AI was created by a team passionate about education and technology. We're committed to continuously improving our AI tutor to help students succeed.
