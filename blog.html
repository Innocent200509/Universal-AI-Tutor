Exams can be stressful, but with the right study strategies, you can succeed. Here are 10 proven tips to help you ace your next exam:

  1. Start Early: Don't cram. Start studying at least 2 weeks before your exam.

  4. Teach Someone Else: Explaining concepts helps solidify your understanding.

  8. Get Enough Sleep: 7-9 hours of sleep improves memory consolidation.

⚡ Pomodoro Technique: Boost Your Focus

March 21, 2026

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that can dramatically improve your productivity. Here's how it works:

  1. Choose a task: Pick what you want to work on.
  2. Set a timer for 25 minutes: Work without interruption.
  3. Take a 5-minute break: Step away, stretch, hydrate.
  4. Repeat 4 times: After 4 Pomodoros, take a longer break (15-30 minutes).
